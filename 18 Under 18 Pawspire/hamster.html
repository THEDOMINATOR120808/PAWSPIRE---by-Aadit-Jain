Hamster



When feeding your hamster fruits or vegetables make sure you give them a good wash before you feed them to your hamster and only feed a small amount each day. A few vegetables suitable for a hamster include carrot, broccoli, cabbage, cauliflower, chicory, spinach, sweet peppers, cucumber, cress, courgette. A few fruits include apple, pear, peach, melon. Hamsters are naturally clean animals who spend as much as 20% of their time grooming themselves regularly and have almost no odour. Therefore, there's no need to bathe them frequently in order to maintain a healthy level of cleanliness.
If your hamster needs something to gnaw on, you can give it cardboard, coconut shells, hay cubes, unbleached loofah, pumice stone, seagrass.
Some pet owners choose to incorporate their hamsters' playtime into their daily schedule; setting aside 20-30 minutes each day for their restless pets to roam about freely outside their cages. Though many people advice a ball or a exercise wheel I don't cause your hamster might get confused on how to stop and get off and might get injured badly.
Hamsters do not require vaccinations. However, hamsters require, at minimum, annual physical examinations, and fecal tests to check for parasites. Older hamsters should be examined twice annually.
